Welcome to My Webpage

I grew up in a small town named Verona, New Jersey. My lifetime has consisted of refining new skills due to my unwavering curiosity of how far my potential can reach. At a young age, I was building legos, reading dystopic thrillers, perfecting different athletics, and becoming a history buff most likely stemming from my Father's teachings. With the philosophical and introspective nature of my childhood, I was bred to see the world through the lens of science and economics. Ultimately, I was accepted to Lehigh University and fulfilled a double major in Economics and Behavioral Neuroscience. My education paved the path for me to focus on my love for technology and the unlimited potential I believe is obtainable for life on Earth. Combining curiosity and rationale, I find it necessary to use technology to improve humanities foresight. Foresight, as much of sixth sense it may seem, can be honed to change the course of the future, and I believe advances in data science will sharpen mankind's decision making for a better tomorrow. My focus now is to get on board the ship of technology by improving my computer skills and maintaining a better work ethic each successive day.
