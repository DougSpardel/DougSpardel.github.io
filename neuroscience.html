Background

The question I asked myself at the start of my higher education was how can I choose a study that encompasses biology, chemistry, psychology, with general computation? My solution was to focus on the brain and how the study of biological and chemical approaches combine to create a physiological computer. Some of the topics to note involve conductances and electostatic force, organic chemistry, genetic sequencing, and a personal favorite, neural data analytics.
